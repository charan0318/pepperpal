## What is Peppercoin?

Peppercoin ($PEPPER) is a community token built on Chiliz Chain, designed to reward the Chiliz community and $CHZ token holders. It brings engagement and community spirit to the Chiliz ecosystem.

**Core Principles:**
- Fair Launch: No pre-minting or ICO. Everyone started with equal opportunity
- Community Driven: Built by the community, for the community
- Decentralized Governance: Token holders vote on treasury and project decisions via Pepper Inc

**What Makes PEPPER Unique:**
- Native to Chiliz Chain (the sports blockchain powering 70+ elite sports teams)
- Governed entirely by the community through Pepper Inc DAO
- No team allocation or insider advantages
- Transparent on-chain treasury management

## Pepper Inc - Governance Portal

Pepper Inc is the decentralized governance system for Peppercoin. It allows token holders to participate in decision-making.

**How It Works:**
1. Stake your PEPPER tokens at peppercoin.com/pepper-inc
2. Voting power is proportional to staked amount
3. Vote on active polls during voting periods
4. Your tokens remain yours (can unstake anytime)

**Current Stats (Live on Website):**
- Pepper Inc Treasury: Community-controlled CHZ funds
- CEX Fund: Dedicated fund for pursuing exchange listings
- Staked PEPPER: Over 1.39 quadrillion PEPPER staked for governance

**Types of Decisions:**
- Treasury allocation (hold, burn, distribute, invest)
- CEX listing fund management
- Community initiatives and programs
- Future development priorities

**Important Notes:**
- Tokens are NOT locked when voting
- You can unstake whenever you want
- One wallet can vote once per poll
- Voting periods are announced in advance

### Governance and Staking

**Q: What is Pepper Inc?**
A: Pepper Inc is the governance portal for PEPPER. By staking your tokens, you can vote on proposals that shape the future of the project and manage the community treasury.

**Q: How do I participate in governance?**
A: Visit peppercoin.com/pepper-inc, connect your wallet, stake your PEPPER, and vote on active polls during voting periods.

**Q: Do my tokens get locked when I stake?**
A: No. You can unstake your PEPPER whenever you want. Voting does not lock your tokens.

**Q: What is the CEX Fund?**
A: The CEX Fund is a community-managed treasury dedicated to pursuing listings on Tier 1 centralized exchanges. The community votes on how to use these funds.

**Q: What is the Pepper Treasury?**
A: The treasury holds CHZ tokens collected for community use. Token holders vote on what to do with it - options include holding, buying PEPPER, burning PEPPER, or funding initiatives.

**Q: Can I vote multiple times on the same poll?**
A: No. Each wallet can vote once per poll.
